About Rovve ISPO

What is an ISPO?

An Initial Stake Pool Offering (ISPO) is a novel token distribution method where users delegate their tokens to a validator and receive new project tokens as rewards instead of the standard staking rewards.

By participating in the Rovve ISPO, you delegate your HASH tokens to our validator and earn RV tokens proportional to your delegation amount and duration, while supporting the Provenance network's security.
