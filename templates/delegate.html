How to Delegate HASH Tokens

Important Information

Delegate your HASH tokens using Provenance Explorer to participate in the ISPO and earn RV tokens.

Follow these steps to delegate:
  1. Visit Provenance Explorer

    Navigate to the Provenance Blockchain Explorer

  2. Connect Your Wallet

    Click "Connect Wallet" in the upper right corner and select your wallet type (Provenance or Figure Wallet)

  3. Find Our Validator

    Click the "Staking" menu option and locate the Rovve validator in the list

  4. Delegate Tokens

    Click "Delegate" next to our validator and enter your desired HASH amount (minimum 100 HASH)

  5. Sign Transaction

    Review and sign the delegation transaction in your wallet

Reward Structure
  • • 5% of total RV token supply allocated to ISPO participants
  • • 60% higher rewards for first 3 months (Jan 15 - Apr 15, 2025)
  • • Rewards based on delegation amount and duration
