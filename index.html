How to Participate

  1. Visit the Provenance Explorer
  2. Connect your Provenance wallet
  3. Find the Rovve validator and click "Delegate"
  4. Enter the amount of HASH to delegate (minimum 100 HASH)
  5. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
Delegate Now

Reward Structure

  • Early Participation Bonus

    Earlier participants receive higher rewards allocation

  • Delegation Size Bonus

    Larger delegations receive proportionally higher rewards

  • Token Distribution

    RV tokens will be distributed at the Token Generation Event (TGE)

Note: Final rewards are calculated at the end of the ISPO period based on total participation and individual contribution metrics.
